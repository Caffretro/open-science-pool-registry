Register a New Data Source

To register a new data source with the HT Phenotyping service, please fill out and submit the form below. An administrator will be notified and they will contact you to finalize your registration.

  • We will send an email to this address when we've processed the registration.
  • We may also send an email to this address if any problems occur with this data source.
  • The data source name should begin with "University_PIName", like "Wisconsin_Spalding".
  • The source name must be composed of only alphabetical characters (A-Z, a-z), digits (0-9), and underscores (_). It may not begin with a digit.
  • You may not get exactly the name you request.
