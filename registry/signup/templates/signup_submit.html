Register a New Data Source

To register a new data source with the HT Phenotyping system, please fill out and submit the form below. An administrator will be notified and they will contact you to finalize your registration.

We will email you at this address to let you know we've processed your signup, and add it to the HT Phenotyping mailing list.
The data source name should be of the form "University_PIName" as in "Wisconsin_Spalding". The source name must be composed of only alphabetical characters (A-Z, a-z), digits (0-9), and underscores (_). It may not begin with a digit. You may not get exactly the name you request.
The location of the data to be synchronized on the source host machine. Leave blank if unknown; we will contact you to discuss further.
